Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Konsou stores your anime list in your own Google Drive. Your data never touches our servers — we don't have any. The developer cannot access your list or your Google account.

What Konsou is

Konsou is a free, open-source anime tracking app for Windows, macOS, and Linux. It stores your list locally and optionally syncs it to your own Google Drive so it stays in sync across your devices.

What data we collect

Konsou requests access to three things from your Google account:

  • Your name and email address — displayed in the app so you can see which account is connected. Never stored on any server.
  • Your profile picture URL — displayed next to your name in Settings.
  • Google Drive appdata folder — a private, hidden folder that only Konsou can read. We use it to store your anime list (list.json) and sync state (meta.json). No other files in your Drive are ever accessed.

This data is stored on your device and in your Google Drive only. It is never sent to Konsou servers, analytics services, or any third party.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not have servers. There is no Konsou backend.
  • We do not collect usage analytics or crash telemetry by default.
  • We do not read, write, or access any file in your Google Drive outside of the private appdata folder.
  • We do not collect payment information. Konsou is free.
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.

Google Drive access

When you connect your Google account, Konsou requests the drive.appdata scope. This scope gives the app access to a single hidden folder (appDataFolder) that:

  • Is invisible in your regular Google Drive view
  • Can only be read by Konsou — no other app or person can access it
  • You can delete at any time at myaccount.google.com → Data & privacy → Data from apps and services you use

Local storage

Konsou stores your list in a SQLite database on your device at the standard app data path for your operating system. This file stays on your machine and is never uploaded anywhere other than your own Google Drive appdata folder when sync is enabled.

Children's privacy

Konsou is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
